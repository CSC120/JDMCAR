NISSAN R35 GTR

With up to 600-hp under its hood, the 2022 Nissan GT-R is a powerful and tenacious-handling sports car that earns its nickname of Godzilla.

The GT-R's engine is a standard twin-turbo 3.8-liter V-6 that makes a mighty 565 horsepower. It hooks up to a six-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive that conspire to put all that power to the pavement.
