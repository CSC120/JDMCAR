TOYOTA SUPRA

The bulbous Mk4 Toyota Supra is a car that showed us that mind-boggling performance numbers are never about sharp looks. The Mk4 Toyota Supra packs an engine capable of pumping out 1000 horses will little to no changes to its internals.

the Mk4 Supra was initially offered with a naturally aspirated iteration of the 3.0L Inline-6 motor churning out 220 hp and was also shared with a duo of Lexus at the time - the SC300 and GS300.But it was the twin-turbo 2JZ that pumped out 320 hp and an impressive 315 lb-ft that helped Toyota make its mark.
